What I enjoy

Cutting narrative and educational clips: pacing, sound design, and simple motion graphics to make ideas clearer and more engaging.

Gaming

What I enjoy

Single-player and co-op titles for relaxation and design inspiration—interest in UI, storytelling, and how games teach systems without a manual.
