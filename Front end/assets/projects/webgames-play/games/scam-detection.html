About Marketplace Watch

Marketplace Watch was founded on a simple principle: to create a safer, more transparent online trading environment for everyone. We believe that trust is the cornerstone of any community, and we are dedicated to building tools and systems that empower users to buy and sell with confidence.

Our platform integrates community-driven vigilance with smart technology to identify and flag suspicious activity.
